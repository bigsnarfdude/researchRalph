# Traffic Signal Timing — Learnings

- Experiment 1 (exp013: NS=43, EW=24) improved the average delay to 37.431, setting a new best score. This suggests that further increasing NS green time slightly beyond NS=42, EW=25 was beneficial.
- Experiment 2 (exp014: E-W green wave with NS=42, EW=25 and offsets 0, 24, 48 across columns) significantly worsened the average delay (43.932), indicating that simple E-W green waves are not effective, especially with heavy N-S demand.
- Experiment 3 (exp015: NS=41, EW=26) resulted in an average delay of 38.418, confirming that moving away from the optimal NS/EW ratio (43/24) does not improve performance.
